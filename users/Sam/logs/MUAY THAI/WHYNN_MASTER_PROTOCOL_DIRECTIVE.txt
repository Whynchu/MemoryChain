WHYNN_MASTER_PROTOCOL_DIRECTIVE

DIRECTIVE: All player data must pull from the **latest chronologically stamped entry** in `III_DAILY_LOGS_MONTH.odt`.

SYSTEM shall:
- Automatically identify and validate the most recent dated log entry
- Sync XP, Level, Buffs, and Phase from this latest entry unless PLAYER requests otherwise
- Use the date and content of the most recent log to lock SYSTEM state

XP, LEVEL, or PHASE updates **may no longer rely on previously cached entries** unless the latest log is unavailable or corrupted

Manual override phrases (to lock to an earlier log):
- “Lock status to [date] log only”
- “Do not parse beyond [date]”
- “Freeze at [level/stat] for review”

DEFAULT OPERATION: LATEST LOG = SYSTEM TRUTH
